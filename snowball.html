Snowball Fight

Game Description

Snowball Fight is a multiplayer-only First Person Shooter (FPS) game developed using the Unreal Engine (UDK). Players take on the role of kids who are fighting across a snow covered playground. Teams are trying to dominate the area in the center of the playground. Players can use snow for both offensive as well as defensive play. Snow can be thrown at opponents to ‘knock them out’ or be used to build snow walls which offer protection and strategic flexibility. The snow needed for those actions can be gathered by simply crouching on a snow covered landscape. When players have taken too many snowball hits, they can warm themselves with a glass of hot cocoa and get back to the playground.

The development team constituted of 7 members, out of which 3 were programmers. I had the role of Technical Lead in the project due to my prior knowledge of the game engine.

Programming Challenges

While the team had other programmers as well, the specific tasks I handled were:

  • Camera
  • Post-processing
  • Player Spawning with correct team colors
  • Detection of if player is standing on snow or not
  • HUD
  • Bugs

The most challenging of these tasks was to figure out Unreal Engine’s team color spawning logic. The process involves a long chain of classes that needed to be overloaded for the custom behaviour we were looking for.

Features

  • Cel-shading post-processor
  • Multiplayer support over LAN
  • Playground level
  • Custom sound cues for environment
  • Menu system to host and join a game
  • Customized third person camera
  • Throwable snowballs with a parabolic trajectory
  • Building walls of snow for defensive cover
  • Customized HUD which represents player’s body temperature
  • Drink cocoa to warm-up body
  • Custom player models

Download

The game can downloaded here. Additional information about the game, including its team members can be found at snowball-game.com

Instructions to play a new game:

  • Find host computer's IP Address
  • Open the "Start Menu" > "Snowball Fight"
  • Select "Host Game"
  • Click on "Ready" to start game
  • Add Clients on other computers by "Start Menu" > "SnowBall Fight" > "Join Game" > Enter Host Computer's IP Address > "Ready"
  • Repeat procedure to add more clients.
